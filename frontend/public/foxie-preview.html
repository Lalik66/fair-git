⚙️ Developer Notes

Show emotion: toggle display:block/none on #fox-eyes-happy, #fox-eyes-cool, #fox-eyes-sleepy, #fox-eyes-surprised. Swap #fox-mouth-path d attribute accordingly.

Wave arm: animate #fox-arm-right with rotate(-40deg) at transform-origin: 132px 148px.

Wag tail: animate #fox-tail with rotate(±12deg) at transform-origin: 62px 185px.

Recolor: override CSS custom properties — --fox-primary, --fox-dark, --fox-light.

Seasonal costume: inject children into #fox-costume group at runtime based on current date.
