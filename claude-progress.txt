PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------
Fair Marketplace is a comprehensive web application for managing recurring city
fairs (Winter and Spring) with:
- Interactive Mapbox-powered maps showing vendor houses and facilities
- 360° panoramic tours using Photo Sphere Viewer
- Vendor application and booking system
- Multi-role access (Admin, Vendor, Visitor)
- Full multi-language support (Azerbaijani and English)
